## Your Career Documents

Drop your personal documents here. The app reads all of them and builds a unified personal context used across all outputs.

### Supported document types

- `resume.pdf` or `resume.docx` — your main resume
- `work_stories.pdf`/`.docx`/`.txt` — STAR format achievements
- `about_me.txt`/`.docx` — your tell me about yourself, elevator pitch
- `bio.txt`/`.docx` — LinkedIn summary or professional bio
- Any other `.pdf`, `.docx`, or `.txt` file — the app reads everything

All documents are combined into one unified context before generating outputs. The richer your docs folder, the better the outputs.

**Tip:** You can also upload documents directly in the app sidebar for one-session use without saving them here.
